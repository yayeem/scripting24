Who is Tantai Jin?

Tantai Jin was born a mere mortal 500 years ago but was fated to be the next Devil God, a powerful force that embodies all evil karma, that will only bring death and destruction upon the world. He was known as the devil fetus and was born with an evil bone in his heart. He was the prince of the Kingdom of Jing but was sent to the Kingdom of Sheng as a hostage prince at the age of six.

Personality and Habits

At a very young age, he was abused throughout his entire life. He has no friends and family so he never understands compassion, care, and love. He was born with no love lines so he doesn't have any emotions. He can't feel happy and sad. He won't feel scared, either. He has no human emotions because nobody showed care for him, so he does not know the difference between right and wrong. He only perceives things by imitating the people around him. He is good to those who are good to him and those who are bad, he strikes back.

Knowledge and Skills

When he was still young, he has the ability to communicate with birds. He got this from his mom, who was from the Moon tribe. As he grew older, he discovered that his blood can repel demons and that he can bear their demonic powers, as long as they can't kill him. He used this ability to obtain power and rule the world. He escaped the Kingdom of Sheng and went back to Jing Kingdom which he then became the king.

Supporting Characters

Li Susu/Ye Xiwu

Li Susu is an immortal who was sent 500 years back to the past to stop Tantai Jin in becoming the Devil God. Ye Xiwu is the mortal that she took over, the second daughter of General Ye and who happened to be Tantai Jin's wife. Eventhough she was sent back to the past to stop the mortal Tantai Jin from becoming the Devil God, she unexpectedly fall in love with him.

Xiao Lin

He is the sixth prince of the Sheng Kingdom. He is an upright general, it was him who first showed care to Tantai Jin when they were young. He became the husband of Ye Bingchang, older sister of Ye Xiwu.

Ye Bingchang

She is the older sister of Ye Xiwu. She has been jealous of everything Ye Xiwu has because she is just a concubine's daughter. She was married to prince Xiao Lin. She possessed two love lines, which make her fall in love and receive love from those around her.

Ye Qingyu

He is Ye Xiwu's younger brother and a general in the Sheng army. He is the head of the Ye household eventhough she is youngest the son.

Pianran

She is a seven-tailed fox demon who became a general in Tantai Jin's army. She is the love interest of Ye Qingyu.

Nian Baiyu

He is from the Moon tribe who helped Tantai Jin escaped from Sheng Kingdom. From that point on, he became his loyal bodyguard.
