Dumaguete City, Philippines

Dumaguete City, located in Negros Oriental, is fondly called the "City of Gentle People" which offers a captivating blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. Silliman University is known for providing quality education in the city. It is also recognized as the first Protestant and American- founded university in the Philippines and Asia. With its coastal charm, friendly locals, and laid- back vibe, Dumaguete attracts both local and international visitors. The name "Dumaguete," from "dumaguet," meaning "to swoop", reflects its history of Moro pirate raids and its irresistible pull on those who visit, often compelling them to stay longer.
