Dumagueteños

As of the 2020 census, Dumaguete City has a population of 134,103 with 32,276 households. The city lives up to its name as the "City of Gentle People," known for its welcoming and friendly locals. Whether you are new or just visiting, you can easily ask for directions or advice without hesitation. As a small city, locals are happy to suggest tourist spots to enhance your experience. You may even meet new friends to share moments with during your stay.

The people of Dumaguete embraced Cebuano language, culture, and customs after the datus from Borneo settled in the area. Roman Catholicism is the main religion, and Cebuano is the primary language spoken.

Dumagueteños



Dumaguete, a hub for entrepreneurs, politicians, and local artists in the Philippines.


Manuel T. Sagarbarria


Affectionately known as "Chiquiting," he holds a degree in BS Chemistry from Silliman University but has made a name for himself in business. He is the President and CEO of several companies and currently serves as the Congressman of Dumaguete City.

Christine Marie Luche Gonzalez


She is a Filipina actress, host, model, and vlogger who has been active in the industry since 2008. She is known for her versatile roles in television and film, as well as her engaging presence on social media.

Dr. Prospero 'Rico' P. Absin


He is a cultural icon in Dumaguete, known for creating the famous Christmas House in the city. His love for Christmas, inspired by his mother, Doña Alicia Pañares Absin, is at the heart of the display.
