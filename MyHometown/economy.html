Gateway to Economic Growth

Dumaguete's main sources of income come from various sectors such as tourism, education, retail, outsourcing services, and technology and IT companies. Some of the companies where I landed my first jobs include Teletech, Qualfon, and Ingenuiti. It was there that I met my first friends in the working world. There is also SPI Global, which has recently rebranded as Straive, along with Sophi and several others. The city is home to several banks, including a branch of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, offering essential financial services. You can also find the Bank of the Philippine Islands, UnionBank, Landbank, Philippine National Bank, BDO, and other local banks offering financial services. Dumaguete is home to several universities, including Silliman University , St. Paul University, Foundation University, and Negros Oriental State University, where I completed my college degree. The city also has various hospitals, such as Negros Oriental Provincial Hospital, Holy Child Hospital, and Silliman University Medical Center, which provide hospitalization services. Ace Dumaguete Doctors, Inc. is the newest hospital to have opened in recent years. Several shopping centers are also available, including Lee Super Plaza and Cang's Inc. Shopping Complex, which were the first malls I visited as a child, along with HyperMart, a sister branch of Lee Super Plaza. Over the years, Robinsons Place Dumaguete, Unitop, and CityMall have emerged in the city, offering a wide range of retail options. The growth of these industries has made Dumaguete a key destination for business and commerce. As a result, the city has become the main center for education, finance, shopping, IT, business, lifestyle, and sports in Negros Oriental.
