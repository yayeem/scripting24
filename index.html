Imee Balderas is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production program at Lethbridge Polytechnic, focused on graphic design, digital video and photography.

Imee is an amateur photographer who enjoys capturing random moments in her free time. She often focuses on nature, sunsets, sunrise, and urban scenes. In addition to photography, she records videos of her travels and loves editing them. To keep her memories alive, she created an Instagram account to share her work and a Facebook page to document her adventures.

Although she is not particularly into music, she loves dancing and participates in simple dance trends. She joined a dance club, where she developed many close friendships. Selectively extroverted, Imee enjoys spending time with her close friends. Her passion for photography was inspired by her brother, while her sister encouraged her love of dancing. As the youngest in the family, she looked up to her siblings for the confidence they provided, enabling her to share those interests alongside them.

Imee worked as a software engineer for eight years. During this time, she served as a localization engineer before transitioning to a UI/UX designer in the Philippines, prior to moving to Canada. Her desire to enhance her skills in design and video editing motivated her to study abroad in a Multimedia Production program. This program aligns perfectly with her interests, and she is eager to learn and embrace new opportunities to develop her skills. She looks forward to what the future holds.

Likes and Hobbies

She enjoyed capturing sunrises and sunsets, often photographing these beautiful moments. She had come to realize that each sunrise or sunset was unique, even when taken from the same spot, as long as it was on a different day.

She also loved going on motorcycle joyrides, relishing the feeling of the wind as she rode. Exploring new places helped her unwind and recharge after a long, stressful week.
